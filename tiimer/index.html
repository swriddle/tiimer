HIIT Timer


Instructions

Time literals

A string in the form "XhYmZs" represents a timed period of X hours, Y minutes, and Z seconds. Any of the different time durations can be present or absent.

Examples

"1m30s" - one 1-minute-30-second lap

Conjunctions

Multiple time periods can be separated with a comma (",")

Examples

"1m,30s" - one one-minute lap followed by one lap of 30 seconds

Grouping

Use parentheses for grouping.

Examples

"(1m)" - one one-minute lap (parentheses have no effect in this context) "((1m))" - one one-minute lap (parentheses may be nested) "(1m),30s" - one one-minute lap followed by one 30-second lap (groupings may be conjuncted)

Repetition

The syntax "EXPRxn", where EXPR is a timing expression, x is the literal "x", and n is an integer greater than 0, means that the grouping should be executed n times, one after another.

Examples

"1mx3" - three one-minute laps "(1m)x3" - three one-minute laps (you can repeat groupings) "1mx3,30s" - three one-minute laps followed by one 30-second lap "(1m,30s)x2" - one-minute lap, then 30-second lap, then one-minute lap, then 30-second lap
